Online Medical Locker :

It'll blow your mind

The Medical Home Portal is a unique source of reliable information about children and youth with special health care needs (CYSHCN), offering a “one-stop shop” for their:

  • Families
  • Physicians and Medical Home teams
  • Emergency Treatment
  • Other Professionals and Caregivers
  • Global Accessibility


We made Medical Locker to provide healthcare related information of the citizens of India to related Doctor's at any place and to serve as a single point of access for consolidated health information.

What You’ll Find in the Portal


Written by parents of Children and Youth with Special Health Care Needs (CYSHCN), this section aims to help families :-

  • Navigate the journey from diagnosis to adulthood
  • All information of patients regarding his personal details,medical history and social history
  • Public Awareness from major spreading diseases
  • Prevention & Wellness Of the leading causes of death in the INDIA
  • Tips for living Healthy life
  • Find valuable information, resources, and support
  • And more...

The Medical Locker


  • Vision: All children and youth with special health care needs (CYSHCN) and their families achieve optimal outcomes through improved availability and quality of information, healthcare, related services, and coordination of care.

    The Medical Locker Portal aims to make this as a single point access for authenticated medical reports and history of patients.

  • Mission : To help professionals and families work together to care for patients.

  • Long-Range Goal : To improve outcomes for youth with special health care needs (CYSHCN) and their families by enhancing the availability and quality of healthcare, related services, and coordination of care.
